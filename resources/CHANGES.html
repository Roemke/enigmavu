The latest version is 1.0 To update, remove the watchface from your watch and then load it again from your locker.

The iOS version has to go through the Apple Store approval process - so there will be a delay before iOS users are able to download the latest version. Wait for the Pebble app to update, then try updating the watchface. (I havn't access to an iphone)

Android users do not need to wait for the Pebble app to update before updating the watchface.
